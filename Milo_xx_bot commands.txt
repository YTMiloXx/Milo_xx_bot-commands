!created (Empty or any existing Twitch username)
> Shows when the Twitch account given was created.

!donate
> Gives milo_xx' donation link. (refunds above €5)

!following (Empty or any existing Twitch username)
> Shows how long the given Twitch account has been following milo_xx.

!hug
> Hugs a random user watching the stream.

!rank (Empty or existing Twitch username)
> Shows the rank in of the given Twitch account in milo_xx' stream.

!recentfollows
> Shows the 3 most recent followers of milo_xx.

!sm or !socialmedia
> Displays all of milo_xx' social media links.

!stats
> Shows the stats of milo_xx' current stream.

!top5hours
> Shows the top 5 users based on hours.

!top5coins
> Shows the top 5 users based on coins.
